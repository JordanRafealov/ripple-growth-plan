The Situation

Ripple’s product is complex — AI operations that automate manual logistics workflows across disconnected systems (TMSs, WMSs, CRMs, ERPs). That’s incredibly powerful, but it’s hard to explain in a LinkedIn message or a Google ad.

The Root Cause

Without a VSL (Video Sales Letter), prospects have no way to self-educate before getting on a call. They land on the website, see text-heavy pages, and bounce. The ones who DO book calls come in cold — the first 15 minutes of every call is spent explaining what Ripple actually does.

Ripple’s product does a lot of things for a lot of people — AI operations across emails, PDFs, spreadsheets, phone calls, across TMSs, WMSs, CRMs, and ERPs. Once the offer and ICP are locked in, everything else — the VSL, the content, the outreach — becomes dramatically more effective because it all speaks to the same person with the same promise.
